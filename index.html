About Us

We are a dublin based hiking group, which consists of like-minded individuals

looking for adventure, wanting to explore more of our beautiful country Ireland,

and to challenge ourselves physically, to maintain an active and healthy lifestyle.

Why join us?

By becoming a member of our group, you will meet other people with shared interests, we welcome all levels from beginner to advanced. Discover Ireland.

HEALTH

Hiking can improve your cardiovascular health, whilst being active in nature is good for your soul. It can be of great benefit to your heart health, and overall helps build strength, especially in your core muscles and develop enhanced balance skills.

WELLNESS

Hiking is not only good for your physical health, but also your mental well-being. It allows us to become present, to calm our nervous system and take time to reflect. Many members join to unplug from their day-to-day lives and enjoy the beauty of our surroundings here in Ireland.

EXPLORE

We explore routes in Dublin, and all over Ireland. As a group, we aim to explore many different trails and terrains, and are always looking for new places to discover. All of our routes are listed with the difficulty level, distance and duration, so members can choose their preferred level or challenge themselves to improve on their current level.

SOCIAL

Meet like-minded people and make friends along the way. Our group is very welcoming, and we have weekly meet-ups for coffee and cake, and some evening events for everyone to get to know each other better. There's a great social element to joining our hiking group along with all of the benefits above.
